We are the Nexus.

"Progressing For The Better"


CS Society is a student organization supervised by the School of Computer Sciences, Universiti Sains Malaysia. We aim to lead CS students' profession by providing all-rounded support through ingenious and practical initiatives and approaches.

We are dedicated to create an innovative and evolving environment that fosters the enrichment and empowerment of each USM Computer Science student's academic, personal, and professional growth.
